Dominion

Dominion is card-based game. On your turn you will draw 5 cards from your deck. The game ends when all "Province" cards have been bought, or 3 action cards have been sold out. The player with the most victory points at the end of the game is the winner.

For each turn, you have 1 action, 1 buy and however many coins are in your hand. When you play an action, your number of actions for the turn decrements. Actions can have +Cards, which means you draw more cards from your deck for this turn. They can have +$, which means you have more coins just for that hand. They can have +Actions, which means you get more actions for the turn. They can have +Buys, which means you can buy extra cards on this turn. They can have +VP, which means you get additional victory point tokens.

When your turn is over, press the "End Turn" button.
